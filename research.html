Research Programme

Optimising Digital Cardiac Rehabilitation

My doctoral research applied the Multiphase Optimisation Strategy (MOST) to enhance digitally delivered cardiac rehabilitation. This programme included a systematic review and meta-analysis, a qualitative exploration of patient experiences, development of a theoretically informed conceptual model, and a 2x2x2 factorial feasibility trial of a digital cardiac rehabilitation intervention.

Multimorbidity & Primary Care (MIDAS Trial)

As a Postdoctoral Researcher at Trinity College Dublin, I contribute to the delivery and evaluation of a multi-site cluster randomised trial addressing patient priorities in multimorbidity across 48 general practices. My role is focuses on managing the one arm of the trial and leading the process evaluation.

International Cardiovascular Risk Research

Through collaboration with the National Institute for Prevention and Cardiovascular Health, I have contributed to international research examining patient and physician perspectives on lipoprotein(a) testing and cardiovascular risk management across seven countries. My role has involved questionnaire development and validation.
