Designing and optimising complex interventions for scalable, patient-centred healthcare.

I am a health services researcher and clinical scientist specialising in digital health, multimorbidity, and cardiovascular prevention. My work integrates behavioural science, trial methodology, and implementation research to enhance the effectiveness and scalability of complex interventions.

My research spans optimisation trials, mixed-methods process evaluation, international cardiovascular studies, and the development of theoretically informed interventions. I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Trinity College Dublin contributing to the MIDAS multi-site trial.
