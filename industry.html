Clinical Trials & Translational Research

My work focuses on the rigorous design, optimisation, and evaluation of complex clinical and behavioural interventions. I have contributed to multi-site trials in primary care and cardiovascular prevention, with responsibilities spanning protocol development, governance oversight, intervention fidelity, and mixed-methods process evaluation.

I am particularly interested in trial methodology, digital health innovation, and the translation of behavioural science into scalable, patient-centred interventions.
